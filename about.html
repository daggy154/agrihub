Growing Together

AgriHub was born from a simple belief: farmers and buyers deserve a direct, transparent, and fair marketplace — no middlemen, no barriers, just honest connections.

🌾 Our Journey

What started as a small cooperative project between 12 farmers in the outskirts of Nakuru has grown into a thriving digital ecosystem. We realized that local harvests, from fresh tomatoes to quality livestock, were losing value due to fragmented supply chains. AgriHub was built to change that — offering a digital space where a farmer in a remote village can directly list produce and a buyer from the city can source with confidence.

Today, AgriHub connects over 200+ verified farmers, hundreds of local buyers, and champions sustainable agriculture across Kenya. Our platform is designed to be simple, mobile-friendly, and deeply rooted in local realities.

🎯 Our Mission

We empower smallholder farmers by eliminating exploitative intermediaries. Our technology gives them price visibility, direct negotiation power, and a trusted reputation system. For buyers — from restaurants to households — we ensure access to fresh, affordable produce with full traceability.

Beyond the marketplace, AgriHub invests in agricultural training, digital literacy, and last-mile logistics. Because when farmers prosper, communities flourish.
