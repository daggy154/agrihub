❓ What's the best planting season for tomatoes in Nakuru?
💡 Long rains (March-May) and short rains (Oct-Dec) are ideal. Ensure well-drained loamy soil and rotate with legumes.
❓ How to reduce post-harvest loss for maize?
💡 Dry to moisture below 13.5%, use hermetic bags (PICS bags), and store in cool, ventilated space. Regular inspection prevents aflatoxin.
